Our Story

Our Heritage

Established in 1985, La Maison D'Or has been a beacon of culinary excellence in the heart of the city. What began as a passionate dream of Chef Jean-Pierre Dubois has evolved into one of the most celebrated fine dining establishments in the region.

Meet Our Chef

With over 30 years of experience in French cuisine, Chef Jean-Pierre Dubois brings his passion for exceptional cooking and innovation to every dish. Trained in Paris and having worked in several Michelin-starred restaurants, he leads our culinary team with expertise and creativity.
